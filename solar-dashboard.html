⑦ Annual Flow Rates: New Solar per Residential Building Completed

New solar commissionings ÷ total new building completions, Wohn- + Nichtwohngebäude (Baufertigstellungen) · per 1,000 new buildings · 2015–2025 · all 16 Bundesländer

💡 What this measures: Each year's new solar installations divided by that year's total new building completions (residential + non-residential), scaled to per 1,000 new buildings. Unlike the stock-based metrics in ③, this is a flow rate: how active solar adoption is relative to ongoing construction output. Note: solar includes retrofits on existing buildings, so the ratio can far exceed 1,000. Hamburg's small completions denominator (1,044 in 2024) amplifies the rate mechanically, making cross-state comparison sensitive to construction volume. Hamburg ranks #16 — last — in this metric in both 2023 and 2024.
