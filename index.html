Effectiveness of SSB Taxes in Reducing Obesity among Children and Adolescents (aged 5–19)

Completed

Term Paper · Global Health Politics · University of Hamburg · March 2024 · Supervisor: Prof. Dr. Max Schaub

Difference-in-differences panel study using WHO Global Health Observatory data from 2000–2022, comparing obesity prevalence in the UK and Ireland against Germany and Switzerland following the introduction of SSB taxes in 2018. ATET estimated at −0.181 pp (p > 0.05).
